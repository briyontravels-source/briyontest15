01SOUTH COAST & HILL COUNTRY EXPERIENCE(5days/4 nights)
details

DAY 1-Bentota To Galle

Start your journey with a relaxing Bentota River Boat Safari. Travel to historic Galle and explore the UNESCO-listed Galle Fort. Enjoy the coastal atmosphere of Unawatuna Beach and stay overnight in Galle.

DAY 2-Galle To Ella

Proceed to the scenic hill town of Ella. Visit the famous Nine Arches Bridge and hike Little Adam’s Peak for breathtaking views. Overnight stay in Ella.

DAY 3-Ella To Nuwara Eliya

Travel to Nuwara Eliya, known as “Little England.” Visit a tea factory, explore Gregory Lake, and enjoy the cool climate. Overnight stay in Nuwara Eliya.

DAY 4-Nuwara Eliya To Kandy

Continue to Kandy. Visit the sacred Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic and enjoy a traditional cultural dance performance. Overnight stay in Kandy.

DAY 5-Kandy To Pinnawala

Optional visit to Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage before returning to Bentota.

02WILDLIFE & ANCIENT HERITAGE (5days/4 nights)
details

Day 1–Yala Safari Experience

Travel to Yala and enjoy an exciting jeep safari at Yala National Park, home to leopards and elephants. Overnight stay in Yala/Tissamaharama.

Day 2–Arugam Bay Beach Escape

Relax at the beautiful beaches of Arugam Bay, famous for surfing and coastal scenery.

Day 3–Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Climb the iconic Sigiriya Rock Fortress and enjoy a traditional village experience. Overnight stay in Sigiriya.

Day 4–Polonnaruwa Ancient City

Explore the ruins of Polonnaruwa and optional Minneriya Safari (seasonal wildlife gathering).

    03SOUTHERN LUXURY & WHALE WATCHING(5 days/4 nights)
    details

    Day 1 – Mirissa Beach Stay

    Travel to Mirissa and relax by the beach. Visit Coconut Tree Hill for sunset views.

    Day 2 – Whale Watching Tour

    Early morning whale watching excursion in Mirissa. Leisure evening by the beach.

    Day 3 – Tangalle Coastal Retreat

    Continue to Tangalle for a peaceful beach experience and luxury stay.

    Day 4 – Udawalawa Safari

    Enjoy a wildlife safari at Udawalawa National Park, famous for elephant sightings.

    Day 5 – Return to Bentota

    Return via the scenic southern coastal road.

    04CULTURE & SCENIC TRAIN JOURNEY(5 days/4 nights)
    details

    Day 1 – Kandy Cultural City

    Visit Kandy and explore the Temple of the Tooth Relic and Royal Botanical Garden.

    Day 2 – Scenic Train to Ella

    Experience one of the world’s most beautiful train journeys from Nanu Oya to Ella.

    Day 3 – Ella Adventure

    Visit Nine Arches Bridge and Ravana Falls. Enjoy short hikes and scenic viewpoints.

    Day 4 – Horton Plains Nature Experience

    Explore Horton Plains National Park and hike to World’s End for stunning panoramic views.

    Day 5 – Return to Bentota

    Relax and transfer back to Bentota for departure.
