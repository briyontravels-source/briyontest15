01CLASSIC SRI LANKA EXPLORER - (7 days/6 nights)
details

Day 1 – Arrival & Relaxation in Negombo

Upon arrival at Bandaranaike International Airport, you will be warmly welcomed by our representative and transferred to Negombo.

  • Check-in at beach hotel
  • Evening beach relaxation
  • Optional lagoon boat tour through mangroves
  • Fresh seafood dinner experience
    •
Overnight stay in Negombo.

Day 2 – Cultural Experience in Kandy

After breakfast, proceed to Kandy, the cultural capital of Sri Lanka.

  • Visit the sacred Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic
  • Explore Kandy Lake
  • Visit a spice garden en route
  • Evening traditional Kandyan cultural dance performance
    •
Overnight stay in Kandy.

Day 3 – Scenic Highlands of Nuwara Eliya

Travel through beautiful mountain roads covered with tea plantations and waterfalls.

  • Visit a working tea factory & tea tasting session
  • Explore Gregory Lake
  • Visit Hakgala Botanical Garden (optional)
  • Experience cool-climate colonial town atmosphere
    •
Overnight stay in Nuwara Eliya.

Day 4 – Adventure in Ella

Enjoy one of the most scenic train rides in the world (Nanu Oya to Ella – optional).

  • Visit the iconic Nine Arches Bridge
  • Hike Little Adam’s Peak
  • Enjoy panoramic mountain views
  • Relax at a scenic café
Overnight stay in Ella.

Day 5 – Wildlife Safari in Yala

Proceed to Yala for an exciting wildlife experience.

  • Afternoon Jeep Safari at Yala National Park
  • Spot leopards, elephants, crocodiles & exotic birds
  • Sunset wildlife photography
Overnight stay near Yala.

Day 6 – Beach Escape in Mirissa

Head to the southern coast for relaxation.

  • Early morning whale watching tour (seasonal)
  • Beach relaxation & swimming
  • Optional surfing lessons
  • Fresh seafood by the ocean
Overnight stay in Mirissa.

Day 7 – Heritage Visit in Galle → Departure

Before departure, explore the historic Dutch colonial city.

  • Visit the UNESCO-listed Galle Fort
  • Walk along ramparts with ocean views
  • Explore boutique shops & cafés
Transfer to airport for departure with unforgettable memories.
